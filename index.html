State-Channel Chatrooms

Messages and interactions are stored in mergeable off-chain graphs, drastically reducing latency and gas costs.

Privacy-Preserving Identity

Using Deterministic Wallet Entropy Inversion (DWEI), users can prove identity and ownership without exposing private keys.

Temporal Compression

No timestamps. Messages are validated by Merkle Temporal Proofs, ensuring order without revealing time.

Self-Healing Contracts

The protocol's Self-Destruct Resurrection (SDR) enables contracts and chatrooms to "die and reborn" without losing history.

Quantum-Safe Encryption

Secured by Quantum Hash Reversal Proofs (QRHRP), future-proof against quantum computing threats.
